Arrays! What are they?

Arrays are a great way to store and access information on a website. But enough talk! Let's see what one looks like, eh?

Let's say that you have a list of car types that you'd like to manipulate on a page. Neat! Here's what an array of those can look like:

let cars = ["Ford", "Honda", "Mercedes", "Tesla"];
Want to know how long that array is? Well, we could just count it, but where's the fun? You can also use a built in JavaScript method called .length! This works on an array, no matter the size!
